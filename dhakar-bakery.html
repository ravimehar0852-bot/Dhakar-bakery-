Our Story

A family tradition, baked into every loaf

2009
Est. · Jodhpur

Born in a Home Kitchen

Dhakar Bakery started as a simple dream in 2009 — our founder Ramesh Dhakar baked his grandmother's bread recipes every Sunday, filling the neighbourhood with an irresistible aroma.

What started with a small counter and a coal-fired oven has grown into a beloved local institution. Today we serve over 300 families every day, but our values haven't changed a bit.

Every bake is still made by hand, with the same love and the same recipes that made our neighbours knock on the door fifteen years ago.
